About Us

Shri Ganesh Rai Inter College

The S.G.R. INTER COLLEGE is located in the area SIDHAUNI of DOBHI. S.G.R. INTER COLLEGE is in the JAUNPUR district of UTTAR PRADESH state. S.G.R. INTER COLLEGE DOBHI was establised in the year 1939. The management of S.G.R. INTER COLLEGE is Government Body. S.G.R. INTER COLLEGE is a Hr. Secondary School. The coeducation status of S.G.R. INTER COLLEGE is Co-educational.

Our community of students, professors, staff and alumni shares a commitment to diligence and make sincere efforts towards the advancement of the society through creativity and innovation. SGR INTER COLLEGE is the temple of knowledge devoted to innovative learning, creativity and effective application of knowledge and values for sustainable development.
